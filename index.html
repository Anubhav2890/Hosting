About Us

Why choose us for the best?

We believe inQualityoverQuantity

The Santiniketan region of West Bengal is renowned for its creatively handcrafted leather handbags and accessories. These bags, available in a wide range of colors, patterns, and sizes with traditional motifs, beautifully showcase India's rich cultural heritage. Each Santiniketan bag is a perfect example of the professional craftsmanship of local artisans.

We make our bags with the finest vegan leathers and with around 100 native artisans among our workfore we deliver the best and authentic.
